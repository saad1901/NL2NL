Describe a chart, the AI builds it

Once you're connected, hit the Dashboard button on any chat page. Describe what you want to see — "monthly revenue trend", "top 10 products by sales" — and the AI writes the query and renders an interactive ECharts visualisation. Bar, line, pie, scatter, radar, funnel. All pinned and saved per database. Expand any chart fullscreen for a presentation-ready view.
